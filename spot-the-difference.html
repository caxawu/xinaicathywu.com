Spot the Difference

Skills: Brainstorming, Prototyping
Tools: Maya, Unity

apk for Oculus Go

The prompt

Use the Oculus Go and build something in VR.

What we chose

Because there are an infinite number of things to do in VR, my partner and I thought of ways in which VR would add to an experience, rather than it being a gimmick. We found our way to spot-the-difference games and thought to reimagining this static 2D game into an animated 3D one.

The goal

To create a delightful and fun experience that is calming and peaceful.

How it works

When the game starts, the player can chose between the actual scene and the differences scene.
In the actual scene, the player can walk around and explore. Almost every object animates—from swaying trees, to lilypads, to a floating hot air balloon. Squirrel, birds, fish, and butterflies add another fun element that gives more life to the scenes.
The player can then head over to the differences scene to try and spot the extra elements. The player has only 5 guesses to try and win the game!
